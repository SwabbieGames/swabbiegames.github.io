🐾 9 Trials of Whiskers

📢 Latest Update

The first day of working on achievements is coming to an end!
Most of the time was spent researching how achievements connect to Steam through its API. By the end of the day, I had created achievement icons for each trial (1-9) and successfully tested adding an achievement in Steamworks.

Over the coming week, I'll be creating art for many more achievement types. After that, I'll implement the achievements in-game and test them on Steam. This will also mark the first time the game interacts with the Steam server, allowing me to track stats—like how many times players have died, both individually and across all players.

There will be six hidden achievements in the game... When earned, they will ignite a torch shining onto their corresponding painting in the first hallway.

🛠️ Latest Patch Notes

**Patch 0.1.1 - Individual Trials Ready**
As an option to running all 9 Trials you can now select a specific trial or even the demo version from the main menu!
Want to improve your consistency on trial 8? No problem! 
Think you have the fastest start in the world at trial 1? You can now prove it!

Choices for Trials are:
- 9 Trials of Whiskers (The full game)
- Demo Version (The first 5 trials)
- Any individual trial 1-9

Separate leaderboards and ghosts for each Trial
-Once you selected what trial(s) you want to run, the leaderboards in the menu and the ghosts used will match the trial(s) you chose.
