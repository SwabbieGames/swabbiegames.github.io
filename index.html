About SwabbieGames

Small studio, focused games.

SwabbieGames is my indie game studio where I create games with a focus on satisfying mechanics, clear goals, and replayable challenges. I started with 9 Trials of Whiskers as a learning project, and I am now building toward larger and more replayable games like Omnicore.
